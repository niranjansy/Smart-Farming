The Problem

Nearly 1/3rd of the Indian GDP contribution is from the agriculture sector. It is the source of income and jobs for a significant part of the country. Our country, India, is known as the land of farming.
There are a lot of challenges that these farmers have to face. Most of the farmers are poor, and many are not farmers by their choice but their family condition and education. Many of them strongly rely on the information passed by their ancestors, which may be wrong, leading to total disasters like a complete failure of crops. Usually, farmers take loans from their local zamindars to survive and end up losing their land because they could not return the loan amount, and in worst cases, they end up suiciding.
These problems are prevalent amongst most farmers across the country due to a lack of education and knowledge of marketing and smart agricultural practices. Using these techniques, the farmers can save themselves a lot of effort and time leading to improvised operations and help them maximize their profits.

The Solution

We propose a website service that helps the farmer keep track of his farm, gets weather updates - local rain reports and crop harvesting-spraying-planting notifications. They can also gain access to crucial aspects of their business, including market data, marketing strategies.
Thus the website helps them gain insight into how they can sell their crops and also manage their farm, which further helps improve operations and maximize profits and can stay upto date with cropping and records will be held by our website for ML based prediction.
Along with the website, we propose a seed planting bot, to minimize them of the effort for planting the same. Thus it saves the farmers a lot of time and can improve the productivity and efficiency for the farmer. Also, many farmers can share a single bot, and the website is similar for all, this makes the model cost-effective. For further scope, the bot contains a microcontroller that can be programmed to make it completely automated using a line following technique and also control the watering of plants using soil moisture sensors and smart IoT techniques.
To analyze the effect, one can start observing low shortage rates of crops, low suicide rates of farmers, and smiling faces of farmers and their families across the country.
